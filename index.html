I am currently a Research Scientist in the IBM Research,China for AI Healthcare. I obtained my PhD degree from the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof.Keith C.C. Chan. Previously, I was a Research Assistant in PolyU. Before coming to Hong Kong, I studied in Chongqing Normal University, where I received my BSc degree from the School of Mathematical and Computer Sciences. My current research focus is on the development and application of data mining, machine learning, and AI techniques to analyze, model and derive actionable insights from real world biomedical and health data.
