Dr.Pengwei Hu

Office: Merck Innovation Hub, Darmstadt, Germany

pengwei.hu[at]merckgroup.com



Dr. Hu is a Senior Manager in the Merck Innovation Hub. Prior to that, he worked for IBM Research as a Research Scientist. He received his Ph.D. from the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2018, advised by Prof. Keith C.C. Chan. During the Ph.D. study, he also worked as a visiting research student at the University of Calgary supervised by Prof. Henry Leung. Previously, he was a researcher in PolyU. Dr. Hu’s main research interest is in machine learning, including AI healthcare and biomedical informatics. Specifically, he is interested in what kind of person will have what kind of disease and the interplay between disease and gene expression. Dr. Hu’s other research interests are in natural language processing and graph mining. He has published more than 50 papers at leading ML and Biomedical Informatics conferences and journals. Dr. Hu co-founded Chongqing XiGang Tech Ltd in 2010.
